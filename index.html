Cordova International School

About Us

Cordova International School is a new venture under Jamia Hasaniyya, a combined spiritual and material educational centre located in Palakkad district, Kerala, which was built by the name of Moulana E.K.Hasan Musliyar, a well-known Muslim leader, scholar and a social reformer. Now Jamia Hasaniyya continues its life by conquering the mountains of victories of educational reformation under the leadership of Kanthapuram A.P.Aboobacker Musliyar and M.P AbduRahman Faizee Marayamangalam. The foundation of Cordova International School was laid by Thajul Ulama Sayid AbduRahman Al-Bukhari. Cordova International School aims at bringing the new generation to the top of the modern society by giving international standard of education combined with spiritual education.

COUR MISSION

CIS provides quality education by offering a srtong international academic programme. Our school also fosters social and emotional maturity and psychological fortitude through the value skill development programmes. nurtures the child to practice creativity as a part of life through creativity skill development programmes. trains the child to be morally upright, responsible and a positive citizen of the multi-cultural international community through know the world programme. develops among the students leadership qualities and public speaking skills through Leadership for Life and Public speaking skill development programme. encourages individual thinking and personal organization through co-curricular programmes. motivates the students to develop competitive spirit and healthy sportsmanship.

OUR VISION

CIS will be recognized as a hallmark of International education, nurturing the youngsters of today as the dynamic leaders of tomorrow in all spheres of life with vibrant potentialities and dedication to serve the world.
